About

PULSE is a global radio platfrom which allows anyone to create a live-stream radio broadcast at which they can display any content (within guidelines). Listeners are able to watch a Creator's content and are able to send comments on the live-stream, and Creator's are able to pin the most important comments and are able to accept calls from any Listener, no matter their location in the world.
